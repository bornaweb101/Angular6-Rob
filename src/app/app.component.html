ABOUT
A family business founded over 50 years ago,  KT Group  has grown into one of Montreal’s leading providers of customs bonded transportation, bonded warehousing and container terminal services. We help small and medium sized businesses punch above their weight by enabling our clients to handle more volume.
Using our game changing integrated technology, our customers can access to up-to- the-minute information on their cargo, be it on a train, in our bonded care, or grounded safely in our yard under 24/7 security. Through hard work, KT has remained a symbol of excellence and integrity in the transport industry.
SERVICES
By combining the latest technology with experienced dispatch and logistics personnel, KT Group is the only transport company in Montreal that offers instant system updates  from both Canadian Pacific and Canadian National Rail.
FLEET OF VEHICLES
We provide intermodal TL/LTL shipments which includes stuffing, destuffing to optimize your supply chain management and reduce our customers’ costs.
SECURITY SEALS
To ensure the safety of your shipment, we secure containers in our yard with specialized security seals imprinted with unique identifiers.
EQUIPMENT
20’, 40’, 45’ box trailers and 40’ reefer trailers container haulage service (20’ to 53’ tandem and triaxles)
SECURE CONTAINER TERMINAL
Our main container yard is located just minutes from CN and CP Rail. With three full container handlers on site, we’re able to process a high volume of containers. As a Canadian Border Services Agency (CBSA) registered sufferance facility, this site is fully fenced and has 24-hour security surveillance. The yard is monitored 24/7 by digital cameras and a trained security guard patrols off hours and weekends.

KT Group can temporarily hold emptied marine containers before using them for export loading. We store, drop, clean and repair containers on-site. Let us take the stress off by returning your surplus empty containers to the ocean lines’ off dock container yard.
DISTRIBUTION
We offer local and national distribution (vanloads or LTL- Canada-wide and cross-border), same-day pickup and delivery.
SUFFERANCE BONDED WAREHOUSING
With drive-in doors, loading docks, and racking receive, we make it easy to consolidate, store and reload cargo in our state-of-the-art warehouse. Rest easy knowing that your merchandise is safe and secure.
